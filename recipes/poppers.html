Bacon Wrapped Jalapeño Poppers

What's better than a jalapeño with cheese? A bacon wrapped jalapeño with cheese! I think we can agree that bacon makes everything better.

This simple snack only requires FOUR ingredients and is great for parties! Use these easy to follow instructions to have a snack everyone would love. below are a list of everything we need and then instructions on how to make them.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 12 jalapeños peppers
  • 12 slices bacon

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Half the jalapeños length wise, remove seeds and membranes. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix cream cheese and cheddar cheese together in a bowl. Fill all jalapeño halves with the cheese mixture.
  3. Put halves together and wrap the jalapeños with a slice of bacon. Prepare a baking sheet and place the peppers on it.
  4. Bake in the oven once preheated. About 25 to 30 minutes or until bacon is crispy.
  5. Once out of the oven it's ready to serve. It's not needed but ranch dressing goes great with these as a dipping sauce!
